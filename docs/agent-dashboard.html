Week 10 · 2026-07-13Women founders in AI startups: funding share
1%
of US VC funding went to all-women founding teams in 2024 — the lowest since 2017, even as the AI-inflated headline hit a record (PitchBook)
Key observations the agent surfaced
  • Female-founded startups took a record 27.7% of US venture value in 2025 — but two-thirds went to AI, and Anthropic + Scale AI alone took >$30B.
  • Strip out those two and female founders raised $42.8B — less than in 2021 or 2022. The record is a composition effect, not broader access.
  • All-women teams got just 1% of US VC (2024); globally, 2.3% of the $289B deployed. Deal counts fell a fourth straight year.
  • The honest gauge is the all-women-team share, not the AI-inflated top-line a single trophy round can move.
